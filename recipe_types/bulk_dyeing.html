Bulk Dyeing

You can utilize the many variants of Coloured Liquefied Mastic Resin to also recolour some of your items!

  • Bulk Red Dyeing
  • Bulk Orange Dyeing
  • Bulk Yellow Dyeing
  • Bulk Green Dyeing
  • Bulk Blue Dyeing
  • Bulk Purple Dyeing

Bulk Red Dyeing

Utilizing the red variant of coloured liquefied mastic resin, you can redye some of your items red!

Some of the items that can be redyed include:

  • Shulker Boxes
  • Wool
  • Glass & Glass Panes
  • Candles
  • Mastic Resin
  • And other related items

As of v1.7, integration for other mods has not been implemented. However, it is (eventually) planned!
