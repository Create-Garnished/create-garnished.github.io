Bulk Dyeing

You can utilize the many variants of Coloured Liquefied Mastic Resin to also recolour some of your items!


Bulk Red Dyeing

Utilizing the red variant of coloured liquefied mastic resin, you can redye some of your items red!

Some of the items that can be redyed include:

  • Shulker Boxes
  • Wool
  • Glass & Glass Panes
  • Candles
  • Mastic Resin
  • And other related items

Future Plans

There is integration planned for other mods that have dyeable objects, but there is no pre-determined update for this.
