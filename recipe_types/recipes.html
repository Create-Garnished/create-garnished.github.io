Bulk Dyeing

Any variant of coloured liquefied mastic resin can be used alongside an Encased Fan, similar to bulk washing or bulk haunting recipes, to redye certain items.

These items include, but are not limited to:

  • Shulker Boxes
  • Wool
  • Glass & Glass Panes
  • Candles
  • Banners
  • Mastic Resin

[2024-03-22] There is integration planned for mods that have dyed or coloured items/blocks (such as Farmer's Delight or Ad Astra).




Bulk Freezing

Utilizing Powder Snow alongside an Encased Fan, you can freeze certain items/blocks into new ones!

You can freeze Powder Obsidian into regular Obsidian, or Powdered Garnish into Sugar, or even the various Mastic Paste variants into some dye of its respective colour!

[2024-03-22]Integration for this recipe may also be added, but there are no current plans at the moment.
