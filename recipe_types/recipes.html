Bulk Dyeing

Any variant of coloured liquefied mastic resin can be used alongside an encased fan, similar to bulk washing or bulk haunting recipes, to redye certain items.

These items include, but are not limited to:

  • Shulker Boxes
  • Wool
  • Glass & Glass Panes
  • Candles
  • Banners
  • Mastic Resin

There is integration planned for mods that have dyed or coloured items/blocks (such as Farmer's Delight or Ad Astra).
