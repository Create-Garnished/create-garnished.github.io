Recipe Types

While Create: Garnished focuses on adding unique and, maybe, exquisite foods, there are also some new ways of creating items!

Bulk Dyeing

Utilizing the many different variants of coloured liquefied mastic resin, you can redye some of your items!

Some of the items that can be redyed include:

  • Shulker Boxes
  • Wool
  • Glass & Glass Panes
  • Candles
  • Mastic Resin
  • And other related items

Future Plans

There is integration planned for other mods that have dyeable objects, but there is no pre-determined update for this.
