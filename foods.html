  • Ungarnished Cashew

    Rating:

    Ungarnished Cashews provide 1 nutrition and 0.1 saturation (relative values) to the consumer.

    Other information

    This food provides Nausea upon consumption.

    Can be obtained by breaking Nut Leaves or Cashew Leaves.

  • Garnished Cashew

    Rating:

    Garnished Cashews provide 4 nutrition and 0.2 saturation (relative values) to the consumer.

    Other information

    Can be obtained by deploying a Garnish Compound onto an Ungarnished Cashew.

    Can be obtained by spouting 250mb of Liquid Garnish onto an Ungarnished Cashew.

  • Garnished Cashew covered in Cinder Flour

    Rating:

    Garnished Cashews provide 4 nutrition and 0.2 saturation (relative values) to the consumer.

    Other information

    Can be obtained by deploying a piece of Cinder Flour onto a Garnished Cashew.

  • Garnished Cashew covered in Melted Cinder Flour

    Rating:

    Garnished Cashews provide 7 nutrition and 0.4 saturation (relative values) to the consumer.

    Other information

    Can be obtained by mixing a Garnished Cashew covered in Cinder Flour with a piece of Nether Wart in a heated basin.

  • Garnished Cashew covered in Melted Cinder Flour with effect(s)

    Rating:

    Garnished Cashews provide 7 nutrition and 0.4 saturation (relative values) to the consumer.

    Other information

    This food provides Speed upon consumption.

    Can be obtained by compacting a Garnished Cashew covered in Cinder Flour with 100mb of Lava, a piece of Nether Wart, a piece of Powdered Garnish, a Salt Compound, and 1 of (mostly) any mineral nugget - Iron, Gold, Zinc, Brass.
