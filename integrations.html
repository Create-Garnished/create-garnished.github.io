Even though Create: Garnished is a food mod itself, there has been an effort in adding integration and compatibility between it and Farmer's Delight.

These integrational items range from expanded meats to slices of your favourite desserts.

  • Phantom Patty
  • Phantom Burger
  • Glow Ink Roll
  • Glow Ink Roll Slice
  • Slice of Pecan Pie

Farmer's Delight isn't the only mod that has some integration and compatibility - as certain materials from other mods have also recieved a unique hatchet that you can utilize in your world.
