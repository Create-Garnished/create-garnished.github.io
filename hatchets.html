Down The Hatchet!

Hatchets have improved attack speed and attack damage when compared to swords, but have less attack speed and attack damage compared to axes.

This tool can break the same blocks as axes, with the addition of Cobwebs.

There are also two enchantments available for Hatchets; Ravaging and Salvaging.


Ravaging grants additional or increased drops from certain hostile mobs (and even certain neutral mobs).

Salvaging grants additional or increased drops from certain passive mobs.

Drops from Hatchets - Nether

Enflamed Mandible can be dropped by Blazes when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Ghast Tendril can be dropped by Ghasts when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Molten Remnant can be dropped by Magma Cubes when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Drops from Hatchets - Snowy Areas

Raw Polar Bear Meat can be dropped by Polar Bears when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Polar Bear Hide can be dropped by Polar Bears when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Numbing Parchment can be dropped by Strays when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.

Drops from Hatchets - Deep Dark

Raw Tenebrous Meat can be dropped by Wardens when using the Ravaging enchantment on a hatchet.
