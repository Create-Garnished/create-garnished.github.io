v1.8 - Refurbished

2024-03-31

Create: Garnished has come a long way since its initial release in June of last year. It's shown in both the content added, as well as the player base. With over 90,000 downloads on Curseforge and over 70,000 downloads on Modrinth, we'll likely reach 100,000 by the first anniversary!

But, what might that mean? Well, I firstly plan on releasing Create: BTTK - a modpack focusing on Create: Garnished as well as allowing for players to expand upon their modding tastes, utilizing mods that aren't commonly in modpacks to enhance the player's experience.

Another thing that I hope to accomplish is to release a first-anniversary update - v1.8, Refurbished. This update will aim to tweak many of the more recent additions while also adding more additions celebrating each update!

Ranging from new fiery foods to succulent treats, you can expect many currently existing features to have a slight overhaul.

v0.9 Tweaks & Additions

v0.9 - Incineration - added many nether additions. While, yes, this was possibly one the biggest content updates that have been released thus far, I feel as though some minor things can be added or adjusted.

  • Nether Vegetation Expansion
    • While the end received a global expansion on its vegetation, the Soul Sand Valley was the only nether biome to have received this same treatment.
    This considered, the Warped and Crimson forests will both be receiving some additional vegetation for the player to find!
    • Sepia Nether Tree (Replanted)
      • While the Sepia wood type will not be experiencing any major changes, Sepia Nether Trees will be able to be found naturally generating similarly to how Crimson or Warped Nether Trees generate
      • The below showcases two different versions of what is (almost) the same location, just with the rates adjusted
    • Pansophical Daisy
      • Can be crushed/cut into Pansophical Daisy Petals
      • Can be used to make certain foods or items
        • Omniscient Stew
        • Warped Brew
        • Fiendish Spore
      • Can be used as an alternative way of obtaining cyan dye
      • Can be found in the Warped Forest biome
    • Incandescent Lily
      • Can be crushed/cut into Incandescent Lily Petals
      • Can be used to make certain foods or items
        • Iniquitous Brew
        • Bottle of Crestfallen Flora
        • Fiendish Spore
      • Can be used as an alternative way of obtaining orange dye
      • Can be found in the Crimson Forest biome
    • Sorrowful Lichen
      • Can be used to make certain foods or items
        • Bottle of Crestfallen Flora
        • Fiendish Spore
      • Can be used as an alternative way of obtaining black dye
      • Can be found in both the Warped Forest and Crimson Forest biomes
      • Can be found occasionally in Basalt Delta biome
  • Ashen Evolution
    • Brittle Dust always seemed like a singular feature with a singular main focus, with very little to offer. Well, let's change that.
    • Brittle Dust
      • Not just Scorchia will be used to make this anymore, but also Basalt and Basalt variants
    • Torrid Blend
      • Used in several foods and items, both new and old
        • Piquant Pretzel
        • Cinder Shroom Roll
        • Blazing Delight
        • Molten Stew
        • Putrid Stew
        • Ghandercken
        • Nopalito Wrap Supreme
        • Explorer's Concoction
      • Can be used as an alternative way of obtaining Cinder Flour
      • Can be made by mixing Brittle Dust and Senile Dust in a heated basin
    • Malodorous Mixture
      • Used in several foods, both new and old
        • Piquant Pretzel
        • Cinder Shroom Roll
        • Blazing Delight
      • Can be made by combining Torrid Blend, Cinder Flour, Molten Remnant, and a Bone in a crafting grid

v1.0 Tweaks & Additions

v1.0 - Desolation - was another relatively large content update, though there could've been some more additions made that can improve the mod.

  • End Game Attributions
    • Whilst there is already vegetation in the end dimension, the mobs of said dimension do not provide much for the player to utilize.
    • Dimmed Scale
      • Can be used to create decorative blocks
      • Can be used in certain foods, both old and new
        • Champion's Omelette
        • Chorus Bowl
      • Can be dropped by the Ender Dragon while using the Ravaging enchantment
      • Can be occasionally dropped by Endermen while using the Ravaging enchantment
    • Ender Dust
      • Can be dropped by Endermen while using the Ravaging enchantment

v1.1 Tweaks & Additions

v1.1 - Snare & Tear - did not include much content. And while, yes, there was a pretty major overhaul on how these features function, I believe that there are still some minor improvements to be made.

  • The Savage Ages
    • While I am pretty happy with how the Ravaging and Salvaging enchantments function, there could be more loot tables added for QOL.
    One thing to note about this set of changes is that some of these changes have been mentioned in previous segments.
    • Skeletons & Skeleton-like Creatures
      • Every skeleton or skeleton-like creature will now have a chance of dropping additional bones
      • Every skeleton or skeleton-like creature will now have a small chance of dropping arrows
      • Every skeleton or skeleton-like creature will now have a rare chance of dropping a bow with varying durability

      • Upon the release of Minecraft 1.21, the new Bogged mob will have a chance of dropping additional mushrooms (or a flat chance of dropping mushrooms if it doesn't drop mushrooms to begin with)

      • If the skeleton-like creature is a hostile mob, then you will be required to have Ravaging. However, if the creature is a mob such as a skeleton horse, then you will be required to have Salvaging
    • Horses, Mules, Donkeys, Llamas, &... Cows?
      • Every horse, mule, donkey, llama, or cow will have a chance to drop additional pieces of leather if you have Salvaging on your tool
      • Cows will have a chance to drop additional Beef with Salvaging on your tool
    • Ender Mobs
      • All ender mobs (except for the shulker) will either have a small chance or a guaranteed chance of dropping Dimmed Scales with Ravaging on your tool
      • Endermen, in particular, have a small chance to drop some pieces of Ender Dust

      • Shulkers will have a rare chance of dropping additional shulker shells
    • Arachnids
      • All arachnid mobs will have an additional chance to drop increased String and Spider Eyes with Ravaging on your tool
      • All arachnid mobs will have a rare chance to drop a Fermented Spider Eye with Ravaging on your tool
    • Feline Friends
      • All cats, no matter the variant, will have a rare chance to drop raw cod, and an even rarer chance to drop cooked cod with Salvaging on your tool
      • All ocelots will also have a rare chance to drop raw cod, though they will never drop cooked cod with Salvaging on your tool
    • Bunnies
      • All rabbits, no matter the variant, will have a chance to drop additional pieces of rabbit hide with Salvaging on your tool
    • Snifflets
      • Sniffers have a chance of dropping leather with Ravaging on your tool
    • Dolphins
      • Dolphins will have a rare chance to drop either a Lustrous Pearl or a Heart of the Sea with Salvaging or Ravaging on your tool, respectively
    • Buzzy Bees
      • Bees will have a rare chance to drop honeycomb with Salvaging on your tool
    • Pigs
      • Any piglin, zombified or not, will have a varying chance of dropping gold ingots with Ravaging on your tool
    • Pigs²
      • Pigs will have a chance to drop additional raw porkchops with Salvaging on your tool
      • Pigs will have a rare chance to drop a Tusk with Ravaging on your tool
      • Hoglins will have a chance to drop a Tusk with Ravaging on your tool
