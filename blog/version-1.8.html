Welcome to the v1.8 update page! This page has been cleared for the time being, but do not worry! The majority of the features listed previously will be re-added to this page with time.


Version 1.8 aims to bring new features relating to previous updates, ranging from the fiery depths of the Nether to the wastelands of the End, you can find new items and blocks, or even some improved features that may have already existed in previous updates!

Return to Sender

Added in this update, new vegetation to the other Nether biomes aside from the Soul Sand Valley!

Pansophical Daisies can generate commonly in Warped Forests. This mysterious flower can be used to make certain foods and can even be made into petals to be made into cyan dye or more foods!

Pansophical Daisy in Warped Forest

Incandescent Lilies can generate commonly in Crimson Forests. This wondrous flower can be used to make certain foods and can even be made into petals to be made into red dye or more foods!

Incandescent Lily in Crimson Forest

Sorrwoful Lichen can generate somewhat rarely in Basalt Deltas. This saddened flora can be used to make certain foods or can be made into white or black dyes.

Sorrowful Lichen can also generate rarely in both Warped and Crimson Forests.

Sorrowful Lichen in Basalt Delta

Sepia Fungus is also receiving a small change, now gaining the ability to generate fully grown in Soul Sand Valleys.

This change was made to hopefully make the process of manually obtaining the Sepia wood type a lot easier. But, it was also made to hopefully make the biome just a little more lively.

Though, don't worry, the standard Sepia Fungus and Soul Roots generation will not be changing alongside this.

Grown Sepia Fungus in Soul Sand Valley

Meet some of the foods that you may encounter in version 1.8!

The Omniscient Stew can be made by combining a Bowl, 2 Pansophical Petals, a Fiendish Spore, and a Senile bone in a crafting grid.

The Warped Brew can be made by combining a Bowl, a Pansophical Daisy, a Pansophical Petal, 2 Crushed Warped Fungus, and a Warped Fungus in a crafting grid.

The Iniquitous Brew can be made by combining a Bowl, an Incandescent Petal, 2 Incandescent Lilies, and 2 Crimson Roots in a crafting grid.

The Piquant Pretzel can be made by combining a piece of Powdered Garnish, an Incandescent Petal, 2 pieces of Torrid Blend, 2 pieces of Malodorous Mixture, and a Salt Compound in a heated mixing basin.

Omniscient Stew, Warped Brew, Iniquitous Brew, and Piquant Pretzel

The Bottled Malodorous Mixture can be made by combining a Glass Bottle and 2 pieces of Malodorous Mixture in a crafting grid.

The Bottle of Crestfallen Flora can be made by combining a Glass bottle, 2 pieces of Torrid Blend, 2 Incandescent Petals, and a Sorrowful Lichen in a crafting grid.

Bottled Malodorous Mixture and Bottle of Crestfallen Flora

Of course, as you may have noticed throughout, some items in these recipes are new!

Senile Dust and some related features have received a slight rework! First, Senile Dust and Brittle Dust have gained a texture redesign to look more unique to other dusts both in the mod and even to other mods.

Torrid Blend can be made by combining a piece of Brittle Dust and a piece of Senile Dust in a heated mixing basin. Torrid Blend has a few different uses, involving foods and even as a renewable source of Cinder Flour.

Malodorous Mixture can be made by combining a piece of Torrid Blend, a piece of Cinder Flour, a Molten Remnant, and a Bone in a crafting grid.

Incandescent Petals and Pansophical Petals can be made by putting a flower of the correct type (Incandescent Lily or Pansophical Petal) into a pair of crushing wheels or grinding it in a millstone.

Fiendish Spore can be made by combining a piece of Brittle Dust, a Sorrowful Lichen, and an Incandescent Petal in a compacting basin.

Fiendish Spore, Brittle Dust, Senile Dust, Torrid Blend, Malodorous Mixture, Senile Bone, Incandescent Petal, and Pansophical Petal

Another change that is coming is related to Senile Spread - which will no longer be obtainable by simply applying a piece of Senile Dust to Bone meal.

Instead, you'll have to obtain Senile Bones, which can be obtained by putting either a Tusk or an Irate Tusk into a pair of crushing wheels. This will also have a small chance of producing Senile Dust, but not reliably enough to utilize in an efficient manner.

Tusks and Irate Tusks can be dropped by Hoglins when using the Ravaging enchantment on your Hatchet. However, Tusks can also rarely be dropped from Pigs when using the Salvaging enchantment on your Hatchet.

Irate Tusk, Senile Bone, and Tusk


Desolate Gateway

The Nether isn't the only dimension that received some changes, as the End did as well!

Aureate Shrubs can generate commonly throughout the End dimension - you may even come across some on the central End Island!

Aureate Shrub in End Dimension (End Barrens)

The fabled Ender Dragon has also received some somewhat minor additions!

The Dimmed Scale is one of these changes, which has a guaranteed chance to drop from the Ender Dragon when using the Ravaging enchantment on your Hatchet.

Additionally, they can also be dropped by Endermen and Endermites (at a heavily decreased rate) when using the Ravaging enchantment on your Hatchet.

Ender Dragon in the End Dimension (End Island)
