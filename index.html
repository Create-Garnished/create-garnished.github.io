What is Create: Garnished?

Create: Garnished is a Create mod addon that adds many new foods, ranging from ordinary treats to alien-like desserts.

To get into the basics of the mod, you'll need to find a Plains biome, which will have nut trees scattered about. Harvesting certain leaves will provide you with any of the 9 nuts: Peanuts, Walnuts, Cashews, Pistachios, Pecans, Chestnuts, Hazelnuts, Almonds, and Macadamias. These can be used in a multitude of recipes and can even be consumed itself. However, upon initial harvesting, they are ungarnished and will provide unsavoury effects to the player.

Progressing

In order to make your nuts better, you must create Liquid Garnish! This fluid can either be spouted onto your ungarnished nuts or turned into Solidified Garnish Blocks, which can be used as a more effective way of garnishing your nuts.


In Solidification

You can create Solidified Garnish Blocks by utilizing one of two recipes.

The first recipe requires you to put 1000 milibuckets (equivalent to one full bucket) of Liquid Garnish, 1 Salt Compound, and 2 pieces of Wheat into a basin to compact.

The alternative recipe, which allows for you to more easily automate Garnish Compound among other items, requires you to instead put 1 Garnish Compound, 1 Salt Compound, 2 pieces of Sugar, and 333mb (1/3 of a bucket, one bucket is equal to 3/3) of Water.
