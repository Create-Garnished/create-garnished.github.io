What is Create: Garnished?

Create: Garnished is a Create mod addon that adds many new foods, ranging from ordinary treats to alien-like desserts.

To get into the basics of the mod, you'll need to find a Plains biome, which will have nut trees scattered about. Harvesting certain leaves will provide you with any of the 9 nuts: Peanuts, Walnuts, Cashews, Pistachios, Pecans, Chestnuts, Hazelnuts, Almonds, and Macadamias. These can be used in a multitude of recipes and can even be consumed itself. However, upon initial harvesting, they are ungarnished and will provide unsavoury effects to the player.

Progressing

In order to make your nuts better, you must create Liquid Garnish! This fluid can either be spouted onto your ungarnished nuts or turned into Solidified Garnish Blocks, which can be used as a more effective way of garnishing your nuts.

In addition, Liquid Garnish can be used to generate Calcite when in a similar formation to a Cobblestone generator, allowing builders to have access to a renewable palette while also allowing for more automation possibilities.

In Solidification

You can create Solidified Garnish Blocks by utilizing one of two recipes.

The first recipe requires you to put 1000 milibuckets (equivalent to one full bucket) of Liquid Garnish, 1 Salt Compound, and 2 pieces of Wheat into a basin to compact.

The alternative recipe, which allows you to more easily automate Garnish Compound among other items, requires you to instead put 1 Garnish Compound, 1 Salt Compound, 2 pieces of Sugar, and 333mb (1/3 of a bucket, one bucket is equal to 3/3) of Water.

Crushing a Solidified Garnish Block will provide you with varying amounts of Garnish Compound, which can be used in recipes, applied to ungarnished nuts, and also be turned into more ingredients.

Feeling a Bit Salty

Salt Compounds are an essential element for many foods, including the automation of many items as well. You can obtain Crushed Salt by crushing (or milling) Limestone, which will also produce varying amounts of Iron Nuggets and Zinc Nuggets as byproducts.

Following this process, you will need to create an assembly line. The first two steps are to use a Mechanical Press, and the last step is to deploy a piece of Flint onto the unprocessed item.

Nut Roundup!

With garnished nuts, you can create 3 different variants, of which have varying amounts of saturation and nutrition.

  • Sweetened - Highest nutrition value of the 3, though has a very low saturation value
  • Chocolate Glazed - Lowested nutrition value of the 3, though has a high saturation value
  • Honeyed - Has the same nutrition value as the Sweetened variant and the same saturation value as the Chocolate Glazed variant.

In addition, you can create Nut Mix and other variations of compacted nut foods.

  • Nut Mix
    • Sweetened - Same general information as the base nuts
    • Chocolate Glazed - Same general information as the base nuts
    • Honeyed - Same general information as the base nuts
  • Garnished Meal
    • Serves as a higher tier of food in the early to mid-game
