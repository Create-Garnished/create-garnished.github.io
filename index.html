  • v1.8 - Refurbished

While Create: Garnished doesn't just add nuts, you will most likely come across them and many of their related features during your playthrough.

There are currently 9 unique types of nuts: Cashews, Walnuts, Pecans, Almonds, Pistachios, Macadamias, Peanuts, Hazelnuts, and Chestnuts. Each of these nuts has similar uses and unique uses, of which you'll encounter as you progress through the game. Some uses require mid to late-game items while others can be easily obtained during the early-game.

But, of course, how do you obtain these nuts? By breaking the Nut Leaves that naturally generate on certain trees that are scattered across the Plains biomes of your world.
